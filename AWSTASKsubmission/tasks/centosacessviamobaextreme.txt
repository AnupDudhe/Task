how to get access of Virtual box centos using mobaextreme

step 1
on our centos m/c 
add port 22 
firewall-cmd --add-port=22 --permanent
yum updateall
systemctl start ssh
systemctl enable ssh

then genrate our ssh public and private key
ssh-keygen
here we will get a private key 
id_rsa
mv this id_rsa private key to /

now go to windows powershell
here run following command on our powershell

scp -r root@192.168.0.3:/id_rsa C:\Users\Anup\Desktop

you will get a id_rsa key on your desktop

now launch moba extreme and add our private in moba extreme and add ip of our centos machine
you will get acess of centos7 m/c.
